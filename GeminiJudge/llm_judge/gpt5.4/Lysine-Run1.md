**Strengths:** Exceptional scientific honesty and rigorous grounding in prebiotic literature. The explicit inclusion of "near-misses," dead-ends, and the infamous cyclization bottleneck makes this a brilliant and realistic map of why lysine is notoriously difficult to synthesize prebiotically. The final biochemical concentration step on montmorillonite is a very clever addition.

**Weaknesses:** By choosing to map the *failures* of lysine synthesis, the network fails to provide a continuous, mechanistically resolved, step-by-step constructive pathway to the target. Relying on a "black box" lumped reaction (spark discharge) for the actual production of lysine defeats the purpose of a detailed reaction network.
